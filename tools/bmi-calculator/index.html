What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measure that uses your height and weight to estimate whether you are underweight, a healthy weight, overweight, or obese.

Formula: BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m²)

Note: BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic tool. Consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
