Extract text from a photo, screenshot or scanned document. Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

How to extract text from an image

  1. Upload a photo, screenshot or scanned document.
  2. Choose the language the text is written in.
  3. Click Extract Text and wait a few seconds while it processes.
  4. Copy the result or download it as a .txt file.

🔒 Your image is processed entirely in your browser. It is never uploaded to any server. The OCR engine (~2-5 MB per language) downloads once per session.
